Dégradés radials

Le classique


background: radial-gradient(#e66465, #9198e5);

Closest Side


background: radial-gradient(closest-side, #e66465, #9198e5);

Circle at


background: radial-gradient(circle at 50%,#e66465, #9198e5);

Closest Corner


background: radial-gradient(closest-corner, #e66465, #9198e5);

Multiple


background: 
    radial-gradient(ellipse at top left, navy, transparent),
    radial-gradient(ellipse at bottom right, crimson, transparent)
;

La syntaxe at n'est pas supportée par Safari.

Multiple background-position


background:url('../img/img-16.jpg') center center / cover no-repeat padding-box border-box red;
background-blend-mode:luminosity;

Ceci n'est pas un dégradé, mais bon...
